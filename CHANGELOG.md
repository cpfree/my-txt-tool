   | myTxtTool.oneSpaceBetweenEnAndCn        | txt-tool: 中英文之间设置一个空格                 |
   | myTxtTool.insertBackquoteBetweenEnAndCn | txt-tool: 中文之间的单个单词两边加上空格和反斜杠 |
   | myTxtTool.suitRow                       | txt-tool: suitRow                                |
   | myTxtTool.wrapLine                      | txt-tool: wrap line                              |
